lens Grid Girl 2017 Character Design 1:8 Illustration
format_align_left

Grid girl is an ongoing personal project, which started with the aim of developing a grid-based character design system. It explores the human body as a grid-based object, constructed from basic geometric shapes, with predefined set of rules.

The proportions of the body are based on a head to body ratio of 1:8. In the structure of the head, the eyes are enlarged and placed well below the equator, resulting in a super-youthful appearance.

lens Telephone Love 2017 Animation 1920 × 1080 px Exhibition
format_align_left

An animated video projection, exhibited at Alhamra Art Council, for the Lahore Music Meet festival.

The animation is based on the popular Pakistani song— ’Telephone Pyar’ (1987) by Nazia Hassan. Which is about a girl who is in love with a strange and mysterious caller. The installation aims to entice the feeling of joy and ecstasy that the girl experiences upon hearing the voice of the stranger.

lens Easy 2020 Inkjet print 174 × 197 in Illustration
format_align_left

Illustrations for large scale murals, displayed at a restaurant called 'Easy', popular for its (unapologetically) high-carb food. The scene depicts three characters, who are tired from hearing: to watch what they eat; to not waste their lives on their phones; to not dress this way or that and so on.

However, in the hedonistic, self indulgent environment, depicted in the illustrations, they can irresponsibly indulge in pleasure, and do whatever they want to do.

lens Red Bull Canvas Cooler 2018 Lenticular print 1920 × 1080 px Exhibition
format_align_left

The installation shows a concept of an artificially intelligent, smart mini-refrigerator. Designed to encourage people to drink and buy more energy drinks.

However, due to its advanced, human-like emotional capability, the self-aware electronic appliance has conflicting feelings about its purpose. The machine begins to malfunction, revealing this internal conflict (displayed through the use of lenticular panels).
