Grid Girl
Self-initiated
2017
Character Design
1:8
Illustration

Grid girl is an ongoing personal project, which started with the aim of developing a grid-based, character design system. It explores the human body as a grid-based object, constructed from basic geometric shapes, and predefined set of rules.

The proportions of the body are based on a head to body ratio of 1:8. In the structure of the head, the eyes are enlarged and placed well below the equator, resulting in a super-youthful appearance.

Telephone Love
Lahore Music Meet
2017
Animation
1920 × 1080 px
Exhibition

An animated video projection, exhibited at Alhamra Art Council, for the Lahore Music Meet festival.

The animation is based on the popular Pakistani song— ’Telephone Pyar’ (1987) by Nazia Hassan. Which is about a girl who is in love with a strange and mysterious caller. The installation aims to entice the feeling of joy and ecstasy that the girl experiences upon hearing the voice of the stranger.
